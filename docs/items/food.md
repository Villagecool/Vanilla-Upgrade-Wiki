## Aloe Vera
![img](../ass-sets/items/aloe_vera.png)
`vc:aloe_vera`
| Component | Value |
|---|---|
|Stackable|Yes (64)|
|Use Duration|0.3|
|Nutrition|1 <img class="halfhunger"/>|
|Saturation|3|

### About: 

**Aloe Vera** is plant that comes from [The Nether](https://minecraft.wiki/w/The_Nether)
- Consuming a stalk will grant <img src="https://minecraft.wiki/images/Fire_Resistance_JE2_BE2.png" class="inline-img"/> [**Fire Resistance**](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Fire_Resistance) for 1 Second
- It is also very quick to eat, so idealy you can burn through a stack of these to get out of a situation like **Lava**
- This is a refrence to how *Real Life* aloe plants reduce pain from burns.

### About: 
**Blue Berries** function very similarly to <img src="https://minecraft.wiki/images/Sweet_Berries_JE1_BE1.png?d29d1" class="inline-img"/> [Sweet Berries](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Sweet_Berries)

**Cocojuice** is a beverage found in [Saloons](/features.html#saloons) and created by a <img src="/ass-sets/items/coconut_slice.png" class="inline-img"/> [Coconut Slice](/items/food.html#coconut-slice) and a <img src="../ass-sets/mobs/sparse.png" class="inline-img"/> [Sparse](/mobs.html#sparse)
